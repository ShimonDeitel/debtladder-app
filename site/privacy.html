Privacy Policy

Debtladder is an offline-first app. All data you enter (Debt Name, Balance, Rate %, and dates) is stored locally on your device in the app's private Application Support directory. Debtladder does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data, and does not use analytics or third-party tracking.

Purchases (subscription or one-time unlock) are processed entirely by Apple's StoreKit; Debtladder never sees your payment details.

If you delete the app, your local data is deleted with it. There is no account, no server, and no data sync.
